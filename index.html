About Me

Howdy! My name is Ryan Mok. I am born and raised in Lincolnwood, IL, which is a northern suburb of Chicago that nearly everyone has never heard of. I am the youngest of 4 children, but am often mistaken to be the oldest due to my height when compared to my siblings. I tend to spend most of my free time either being with my childhood friends, going to the gym, cooking, playing video games, or playing my saxophone.

I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2014 where I received my Bachelor's of Fine Arts in Music; I was and still am a fairly large music nerd. I was very active in the athletic bands program which included a Marching, Volleyball, and Basketball Band that performed at every home game; I joined mostly becasue a lot of my friends were also in them. University ensembles aside, I started an ad-hoc ensemble known as Sixth Street Brass. Most of our music can be classified as "riot-jazz", but we also wrote arrangements of popular music such as "No-Diggity" by Blackstreet (we found a way to make it work). For the most part I know pretty much nothing when it comes to web design and coding, but I'm very determined to learn as much as I possibly can!
